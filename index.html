The Best Movies According to Jehad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lego Movie (2014)

The Lego Movie" is a delightful animated film that successfully combines humor and creativity.

The Lego Batman Movie (2017)

A comedic and visually inventive animated film that stands out as a delightful addition to the Lego cinematic universe. The film follows Batman as he navigates the challenges of being a superhero and a loner.

Wall-E

The film follows a solitary robot named WALL-E on a future, uninhabitable, deserted Earth in 2805, left to clean up garbage.

Planes (2013)

An animated film that takes audiences on a high-flying adventure. the story centers around Dusty Crophopper, a small-town crop-dusting plane with dreams of becoming a racer.

Over the Hedge (2006)

lively animated comedy that follows a group of woodland creatures led by a scheming raccoon named RJ. The movie balances humor for both kids and adults.
